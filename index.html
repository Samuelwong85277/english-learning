🔊 Phonics Guide
① Find the Vowels

Every syllable has a vowel. Look for a, e, i, o, u (and sometimes y).

② Split the Word

Cut between consonants around each vowel:
in|qui|ry → in·qui·ry

③ Silent e Rule

When a word ends with e, the vowel before it says its name:
ta|ble → /ˈteɪ·bəl/, quo|ta|tion → /kwoʊ·ˈteɪ·ʃən/

④ Vowel Pairs

Two vowels together → first one says its name:
lead (ea→/iː/), ship (no pair, i→/ɪ/)

⑤ R-controlled

Vowel + r = special sound:
or→/ɔːr/, er→/ɜːr/, ir→/ɜːr/
